Baron of Mauá

The greatest brazilian businessman

Who was the Baron

Irineu Evangelista de Sousa (1813-1889) most known as Barão de mauáwas an important Brazilian banker, industrialist and politician was one of the pioneers of industrialization in 19th century Brazil.

Reasons why he was a great person

  • Undertook the construction of the first Brazilian railroad. Built in the State of Rio de Janeiro, it was named Estrada de Ferro Mauá.

  • He was fervently against slavery, paying for the freedom of countless slaves

  • It was against the Paraguayan War or the Triple Alliance War, in his words the cursed war
